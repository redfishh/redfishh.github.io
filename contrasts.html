Contrasts: Painting a Sentimental Tribute (academic)

role

Director of Videography, Graphic Designer

timeframe

three months

A poetry video + documentary style response combination made in collaboration with Donna Seto, a local Vancouver poet, to adapt her poem, Contrasts, to film.

This film project was made for the course IAT344: Moving Images. In this project, my team and I was given the opportunity to collaborate with a local poet and create a short documentary regarding the meaning of the poem. In addition to videography, I was to design our press kit materials.

  • Awards won:
  • IAT344 Fall 2022 Best Sound (Documentary)
  • IAT344 Fall 2022 Audience Choice (Documentary)
  • IAT344 Fall 2022 Best Press Kit (Across All Projects)

tools used

  • Final Cut Pro
  • Figma
  • Sony Digital Cameras
