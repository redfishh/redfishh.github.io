Publication Layout (academic)

timeframe

roughly three months

For the course PUB332: Graphic Design in Transition: Print and Digital Periodicals, I was tasked with creating a pitch for a magazine publication concept, as well as creating a cover design, feature editorial design, web design mockups, and advertisement mockups. This project spanned the entire semester of Spring 2021 and simulated the process of establishing a magazine publication in the field. Placeholder text was used for this project as a mandatory requirement.
